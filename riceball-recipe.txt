Ingredients:
 - 1   cup rice
 - 1   box seawead
 - 2   spoon dried meat floss
 - 1/3 cup olive oil
 - 1/2 plate spinach

Directions:
  1. Roll cooked rice by a stick and shape it into a ball by hand
  2. Fry the seawead and dried meat floss in olive oil
  3. Mixed the fried seawead and meat floss into the rice ball by squashing all them together
  4. Cover the rice ball with spinach
